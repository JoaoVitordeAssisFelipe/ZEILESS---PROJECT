GOLDENEAS

GOLDENEAS was born from a legend. It is said that, in a small village in the Swiss mountains, there was a craftsman named Elias Goldener, famous for his ability to create watches with impeccable precision. His works were so refined that he was said to not only measure time, but shape it. However, Elias never produced watches for sale; he believed that the true value of a piece lay in its personal connection to the owner.

After his death in 1920, his grandson, Johann Goldener, decided to honor his grandfather's legacy and created the first collection of GOLDENEAS watches, named after his heritage. The brand was born with the aim of combining the art and precision inherited from Elias with a touch of modernity and innovation. GOLDENEAS watches have since become symbols of luxury and tradition, made by hand and with noble materials, such as gold, polished stainless steel and sapphire crystals.

Each GOLDENEAS piece carries with it the story of a lineage of master watchmakers and dedication to time as a true work of art. The sophisticated design and millimeter precision of their mechanisms mean that these watches transcend generations, being passed from father to son, like a timeless treasure.

BLACKOUT

BLACKOUT was born from the daring to break standards and redefine the concept of watchmaking. Founded by a group of young innovators in a futuristic metropolis, the brand emerged with a unique proposal: to create minimalist watches that were at the same time elegant and carried a touch of mystery. Inspired by urban nights and the aesthetics of the unknown, BLACKOUT brought to the world watches with a completely monochromatic design, where black dominated every detail.

The concept behind BLACKOUT watches was simple, but revolutionary: creating pieces that symbolize the passage of time without distractions, standing out for their sophistication and lack of excess. Each watch, made from high-quality materials such as anodized steel and tempered glass, is designed to be durable yet elegant, reflecting a modern, urban lifestyle.

Over the years, BLACKOUT has become an iconic brand among lovers of contemporary design. His watches, in addition to being objects of desire, represent a philosophy of life: seeing beauty in simplicity and finding the extraordinary in the essential.
